What is Depression?

According to APA, depression is a negative affective state, ranging from unhappiness and discontent to an extreme feeling of sadness, pessimism, and despondency, that interferes with daily life. It has origins in our body, genes, temperament, pattern of thinking, way of handling emotions, family history, relationships, past and current experiences.

It is also called as mood or affective disorders. It is a threatening condition that adversely affects the way person thinks, feels, acts and behaves. It can appear suddenly with no apparent reason or it may triggered by illness, hormonal imbalance or even of seasons.
